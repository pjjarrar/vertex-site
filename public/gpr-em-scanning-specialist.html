Houston, Texas

Overview

Company: Vertex GPR
Job Title: GPR/EM Scanning Specialist
Location: Greater Houston Area
Job Status: Full-Time

About Vertex GPR:

Vertex GPR, established in 2018, is a small but rapidly growing company specializing in concrete scanning and utility locating. We pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service, streamlined communication, and knowledgeable team. We aim to provide the highest quality service while ensuring a superior client and employee relationship.

Position Overview:

We are seeking a motivated and skilled candidate to join our team. The core responsibilities for this position include performing onsite concrete scanning and utility locating work, managing project portfolios, and ensuring customer satisfaction. This position requires strong communication skills and the ability to work closely with both customers and peers. The ideal candidate will have 1 to 2 years of relevant experience.

Key Responsibilities:
  • forming subsurface utility locating and or concrete scanning tasks on project sites using but not limited to ground penetrating radar and electromagnetic detection technologies.
    • Concrete Scanning: Tasks include scanning concrete structures for structure reinforcing such as rebar, post tension cables, and wire mesh. Scan for additional hazards such as conduits. Physically mark detected items onto the scanned concrete structure and generate a written report.
    • Subsurface Utility Locating: Tasks include accurately locating underground utilities such as electrical, communications, gas, sewer, and water. Once located, mark detected items directly onto the ground, collect data with GPS, and generate a written report.
  • Knowledgably respond to customer inquiries.
  • Maintain detailed and organized project portfolios from start to finish.
  • Driving to and from project sites while ensuring the safety of company vehicle and equipment.
Qualifications:
  • Ability to learn ground penetrating radar and electromagnetic technologies (if required).
  • Physically capable of carrying 60lbs.
  • Ability to walk long distances.
  • Ability to work on knees for extended periods of time.
  • Able to work nights and weekends if required by the schedule.
  • Ability to travel out of town for a few days per month.
  • Valid driver’s license and safe driving record.
  • Must live within 50 miles of the applied for position.
  • Able to pass a drug screen.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Able to adapt to various onsite conditions, i.e. Scope change, weather, and customer’s objectives, etc.
  • Basic Microsoft 365 and GoogleEarth skills.
  • U.S. Citizen.
Additionally Preferred Qualifications:
  • 1 to 2 years of experience with Utility Locating and Concrete Scanning.
  • OSHA 30, TWIC and Lift Training.

UBICACIÓN DE SERVICIOS SUBTERRÁNEOS

Vertex GPR ofrece servicios para la ubicación de servicios subterráneos mediante la utilización de un radar de penetración de suelo (GPR) y detección electromagnética (EM). La ubicación de servicios subterráneos es muy importante para la perforación de suelo, la excavación, la perforación direccional, la iluminación natural, la conformidad de la obra terminada y el diseño actualizado.

Servicios Subterráneos Para Considerar Antes de Comenzar Su Proyecto:
Eléctrico, gas, comunicaciones, alcantarillado, desagüe pluvial, vapor, agua potable, servicios contra incendios e irrigación.

ESCANEO DE CONCRETO

A la hora de decidir dónde se va a taladrar o hacer cortes, nuestro servicio de escaneo de concreto proporciona información crucial. Identificando los peligros potenciales en tiempo real crea condiciones de trabajo más seguras, reduce el tiempo perdido, los excesos de costos, y ayuda en el diseño del proyecto.

¿Qué podemos ubicar?
Cables postensados, conductos incrustados, grosor del concreto, varilla, malla de alambre y formación de alvéolos/espacios vacíos.
