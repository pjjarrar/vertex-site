About The Company

Vertex GPR is dedicated to providing our customers with the top tier industry experience. Whether your project is in need of concrete scanning or a subsurface utility locate, we are confident that our commitment, knowledge and proactive approach will put you one step closer to a successful project.

Direct Line of Communication

At Vertex GPR we understand that communication is power. How important do we feel it is? When you call or email, a GPR Operator will quote and schedule your project during that call or in the reply email. One call, that’s all it takes. The GPR Operator responsible for your project will manage it from start to finish.

Project Reporting

Vertex GPR understands that the most effective way to mitigate an unwanted strike is to have critical items marked onsite. We also realize that proper reporting of findings is equally as important for safety and project planning. Whether your project is a subsurface utility locate or a concrete scan, we can generate various reports ranging from a detailed written description of findings to a CAD or map.
