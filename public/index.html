Direct Line of Communication

Project Reporting

Vertex GPR understands that the most effective way to mitigate an unwanted strike is to have critical items marked onsite. We also realize that proper reporting of findings is equally as important for safety and project planning. Whether your project is a subsurface utility locate or a concrete scan, we can generate various reports ranging from a detailed written description of findings to a CAD or map.

Comprehensive Approach Strategy

Vertex GPR believes all projects should be driven by a clear and direct objective. Part of our strategy it is crucial to understand four aspects.

  • What needs to be located?
  • Why does it need to be located?
  • What is the overall goal?
  • What is your expected outcome?


These simple points are the foundation upon which successful projects are built. The more Vertex GPR understands these points the better the outcome. Improved results can include the following:

  • A safer work environment
  • Effective results
  • Decreased onsite time
  • Decreased or no standby time
  • Proper reporting
  • Less site visits
  • Reduction in overall costs
